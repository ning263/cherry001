# MVP

## Purpose

The MVP should prove whether the project can create a better first encounter with a literary work than a normal AI summary.

## First Stage

The first stage should focus on content before product surface.

The key question is:

Can the system transform a work into a story-first, causally coherent, emotionally continuous experience?

## In Scope

- One or a few representative works.
- Story archetype analysis.
- Story, causal, and emotional nodes.
- A generated narrative experience.
- Basic post-experience discussion prompts.

## Out Of Scope

- Full social features.
- Large library coverage.
- Complex personalization.
- Replacing original reading.
- Final product monetization model.

## Success Criteria

The MVP succeeds if users feel:

- They entered the work rather than only heard a summary.
- They understand why events and emotions unfold.
- They want to read, re-read, or discuss the original work.
- AI helped them approach the work without closing its meaning.
