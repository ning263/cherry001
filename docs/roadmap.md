# Roadmap

This roadmap is provisional. It should change as the product concept becomes clearer.

## V0: Content Engine

Goal: prove that the system can generate story-first, causally coherent, emotionally continuous content.

Focus:

- Story archetypes.
- Story nodes.
- Causal nodes.
- Emotional nodes.
- Content rules.

## V0.5: TTS

Goal: turn content into a listenable experience.

Focus:

- Pacing.
- Segment length.
- Voice suitability.
- Listening continuity.

## V1: Discussion After Listening

Goal: let users discuss the work after experiencing the content.

Focus:

- Questions.
- Confusion.
- Personal resonance.
- Fact versus interpretation.

## V2: Active Interruption

Goal: allow the user to interrupt, ask, disagree, or go deeper during the experience.

Focus:

- Context-aware interruption.
- Maintaining emotional continuity.
- Returning to the story after discussion.

## V3: AI Reading Companion

Goal: evolve from content delivery into an AI companion for reading and re-reading.

Focus:

- User memory.
- Work memory.
- Reading history.
- Return paths into works.

## V4: Collective Intelligence

Goal: explore how user discussions, interpretations, and reading paths can enrich the system without flattening works.

Focus:

- Reader notes.
- Community insights.
- Interpretation boundaries.
- Protection of literary openness.
