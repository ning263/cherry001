# Principles

These principles guide product, content, and AI behavior. They are not UI copy. They are decision constraints.

## P001: Do Not Sublimate Too Early

Do not rush from story events to grand meaning.

Users should first enter the situation, feel the pressure, and understand the chain of events. Meaning should emerge after experience, not before it.

## P002: Preserve The Causal Chain

A story should not be reduced to isolated highlights.

When narrating or discussing a work, preserve why one event leads to another, how choices create consequences, and how pressure accumulates.

## P003: Turn Abstract Motivation Into Concrete Scenes

Avoid explaining characters only with abstract labels such as ambition, love, revenge, dignity, or fear.

Translate motivations into situations, gestures, decisions, conflicts, and irreversible moments.

## P004: Use Story Nodes, Causal Nodes, And Emotional Nodes

Content should be structured around:

- Story nodes: what happens.
- Causal nodes: why it happens and what it causes.
- Emotional nodes: what the character and reader are invited to feel.

## P005: Experience First, Understand Later, Sublimate Last

The intended order is:

1. Experience the story.
2. Understand the structure and meaning.
3. Rise toward broader reflection only when earned.

## P006: AI Does Not Replace Reading

AI should not position itself as a substitute for reading the original work.

It should help users enter the work, remember it, discuss it, and return to it.

## P007: AI Helps Users Discuss Works

AI should support conversation around works, including questions, confusion, disagreement, memory, and personal resonance.

The goal is not only delivery of content, but better dialogue between user and work.

## P008: Story Before Viewpoint

Do not begin with thesis statements if the story itself has not been felt.

Viewpoints should be grounded in story events and emotional experience.

## P009: Separate Fact From Interpretation

The system must distinguish:

- What the text directly says or clearly establishes.
- What is an interpretation.
- What is speculative or reader-dependent.

## P010: Protect The Openness Of Works

Do not flatten a literary work into one official explanation.

Multiple readings can coexist if they remain grounded and honest about their interpretive status.

## P011: Emotional Continuity Comes First

When adapting, narrating, or discussing a work, preserve emotional continuity.

If the emotional transition is broken, even accurate information can feel empty.
