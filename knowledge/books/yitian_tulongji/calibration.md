# Calibration: 倚天屠龙记

This file records a calibration example for the content engine. It is not a final analysis of the novel.

## Working Archetype

`倚天屠龙记` may be approached as:

```text
少年救世主神话
↓
一个天性善良的人，被不断推上权力中心以后，如何保持初心
```

Another close formulation:

```text
一个从小见过太多仇恨的人，始终没有让自己变成仇恨的一部分
```

## Bad Summary To Reject

A weak summary would say:

```text
张无忌学神功、当教主、打败坏人、最后归隐。
```

This misses the real emotional structure.

## Better Entry

The story should not begin by announcing themes. It should begin with the inherited wound:

- 张翠山 and 殷素素 return to the Central Plains.
- Everyone wants the secret of the 屠龙刀.
- The so-called righteous sects are also driven by greed and fear.
- 张无忌 watches his parents die.
- From then on, he cannot easily believe the simple division between 正派 and 邪派.

## Causal Spine

The key causal chain:

```text
父母双亡
↓
正邪边界被打碎
↓
身中寒毒，背负必死命运
↓
坠落深谷，获得九阳神功
↓
光明顶站出来阻止互相杀戮
↓
被推上明教教主的位置
↓
不断面对权力、仇恨、爱情和历史选择
```

## Emotional Center

张无忌's central trait should not be reduced to "优柔寡断".

A stronger reading:

```text
在一个所有人都想赢的世界里，他一直在想：能不能不要死人？
```

His softness is not only weakness. It comes from seeing too early how people are killed by faction, revenge, and greed.

## Key Scene: 光明顶

光明顶 is a crucial scene because 张无忌 first truly stands out.

The point is not that he defeats everyone. The point is that he sees both sides:

- Both sides have good people.
- Both sides have bad people.
- Both sides have grievances.
- Both sides are ready to continue killing.

So his action is not conquest. It is interruption.

## Comparative Frame

The comparison with `The Count of Monte Cristo` is useful:

```text
唐泰斯:
被陷害 -> 入狱 -> 遇见法利亚 -> 获得力量

张无忌:
父母双亡 -> 身中寒毒 -> 坠落深谷 -> 获得九阳神功
```

Both return from the abyss.

But their life questions diverge:

```text
唐泰斯: 如果我拥有力量，我该如何复仇？
张无忌: 如果我拥有力量，我能不能停止仇恨？
```

## Style Notes

For this project, the better version uses:

- Short conversational lines.
- Clear rejection of shallow summary.
- Archetype discovery before full interpretation.
- Concrete scenes before concepts.
- Emotional question as the ending.

Avoid:

- Overly polished essay paragraphs.
- Naming "power" or "authority" too early.
- Compressing the whole novel into a neat conceptual label.
- Treating the interpretation as final.
